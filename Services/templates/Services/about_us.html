Smart Gram Panchayath is a web application that is specially designed to establish an environment, wherein village public can easily communicate with the village councils. Our application features: administrator, PDO and village public. Administrator will handle the whole website and manage the activities of users, PDO will update panchayath services, application, payments and complaints filled by public. The village public will register to the application and avail for the facilities. This post has complete information of Smart Gram Panchayath Project Documentation.
